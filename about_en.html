About Us

Beijing Starcore Venture Group Inc. , with headquarter in Beijing, China, is planning to expand branches in Shanghai, China and the United States. Since its establishment, Beijing Starcore Venture Group Inc. actively cooperates with government guiding funds relying on its strong social network; introduces global strategic partners with focuses on integrated circuits, mobile Internet, online education and other sectors. The company covers the international M & A as the main investment direction and is devoted to consolidation and innovation of emerging technology industry. In order to adapt to the development of new industry, strategic investors must develop far-sighted top-level design, exceptional industry integration ability, and creative business model. Our team of experts and partners, equipped with extensive professional knowledge and practical experience, aims at both global leading technology import and domestic industry consolidation and advancement which is able to assist Chinese companies in the new industry to gain the competitive edge world-wide. This is as the pursuit of the company and meanwhile as the mission entrusted to us by the nation.
